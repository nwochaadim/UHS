About Us

Uyo High School was commissioned over 20 years ago with one primary motive which is to instill discipline, knowledge and a sense of direction in all its students. We have a proper learning environment with good enough learning facilities for your wards to study and learn in a conducive environment. We alse have seasoned teachers and facilitators that are always ready to give their best to ensure your wards get the appropriate knowledge and skills.
